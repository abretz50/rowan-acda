President: Hanna Shein

Hanna Shein is a senior Music Education major with a passion for building community through choral music. As ACDA President, she is committed to fostering leadership, collaboration, and growth for future music educators. Outside of ACDA, Hanna sings in Concert Choir and Voces and works closely with area school music programs.

Vice President: Adam Bretz

Adam Bretz is an Electrical and Computer Engineering major with a minor in Music. He brings a unique combination of technical expertise and musical experience to the Vice President role. Adam is an accomplished organist and pit musician, and he’s passionate about combining music, technology, and outreach to support fellow students.

Treasurer: Jessenia Zavala

Jessenia Zavala is a dedicated Music Education student who ensures ACDA’s operations run smoothly behind the scenes. Secretary: Makayli Matias

As Secretary, she keeps everyone informed and organized, handling communication and documentation. Social Media Chair: Emma Seidel

Emma Seidel is a vibrant Music Education student who brings creativity and enthusiasm to her role as Social Media Chair. She manages ACDA’s online presence, promoting events and celebrating member achievements. Emma is committed to creating a welcoming and inclusive digital space for the chapter.

SGA Representative: Cameron Hyson

Cameron Hyson serves as the bridge between ACDA and Rowan’s Student Government Association. As a detail-oriented and thoughtful leader, Cameron ensures the chapter stays connected to campus-wide initiatives. He’s also a strong advocate for student voice in music education policy and programming.
