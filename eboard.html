President - Hanna Shein

Hanna Shein is a junior Vocal Performance and Music Education double major who can always be found singing! She is incredibly passionate about the world of choral arts, and is so happy to have found a home in Rowan’s ACDA chapter. She has performed in a number of different choral ensembles, including: NJ All State Mixed Choir, NAfME All Eastern Treble Choir, Honors Performance Series @ Carnegie Hall and many more. Some of her favorite memories with the Rowan Concert Choir include performing at Carnegie Hall and also performing with the fabulous Chanticleer. Hanna lives on the stage, and recently performed as Hansel in Rowan Opera Company’s production of Engelbert Humperdinck’s Hansel and Gretel. When she isn’t singing, she enjoys curling up with a book or obsessing over her cat Luna.

Vice President - Adam Bretz

Adam Bretz is an Electrical & Computer Engineering Major with a minor in music (Organ Performance). He supports the president and helps with club activities, and also maintains the chapter's website.

Treasurer - Jessenia Zavala

Jessenia Zavala is a junior Vocal Music Education major who oversees the club's finances, plans fundraisers, and leads engaging meetings. She's also involved in ACDA, Tri Alpha, and sings with the Concert Choir.

Secretary - Makayli Matias

Makayli Matias is a sophomore studying Vocal Music Education. She manages communication and tracks point totals for NAfME and ACDA. She sings with the Concert Choir and Voces ensemble and is a member of the Opera Company.

Social Media Coordinator - Emma Seidel

Emma Seidel is a music education major who keeps the Rowan NAfME social media platforms updated with news and events. She co-manages the chapter's website and communicates with neighboring collegiate chapters.

Senator - Cameron Hyson

Cameron Hyson is a Music Education major who represents the club's interests to the school. He's involved in the Rowan Concert Choir and the American Choral Directors Association, and is available to discuss questions or concerns about events, professional development, or general meetings.
