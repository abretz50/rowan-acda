Hanna Shein

President | sheinh29@rowan.edu

Hanna Shein is a junior Vocal Performance and Music Education double major who can always be found singing! She is incredibly passionate about the world of choral arts, and is so happy to have found a home in Rowan’s ACDA chapter. She has performed in a number of different choral ensembles, including: NJ All State Mixed Choir, NAfME All Eastern Treble Choir, Honors Performance Series @ Carnegie Hall and many more. Some of her favorite memories with the Rowan Concert Choir include performing at Carnegie Hall and also performing with the fabulous Chanticleer. Hanna lives on the stage, and recently performed as Hansel in Rowan Opera Company’s production of Engelbert Humperdinck’s Hansel and Gretel. When she isn’t singing, she enjoys curling up with a book or obsessing over her cat Luna.

Jessenia Zavala

Treasurer | zavala34@students.rowan.edu

Jessenia Zavala is a junior Vocal Music Education major at Rowan University and is excited to be serving as Treasurer for ACDA this year! In this role, she will manage the chapter’s finances, help organize choir-related fundraisers, and oversee budgeting for club trips and events, including attending opera performances. Jessenia also serves as Treasurer for NAfME, is a proud member of Tri Alpha, sings with Concert Choir, and studies voice with Professor Marian Stieber. She has previously performed with Rowan’s Opera Company, appearing as the Sandman in Hansel and Gretel and performing in the Fall Opera Scenes. Jessenia has also performed in several productions at Ocean County College, including All Shook Up, Kiss Me Kate, and Broadway for the Holidays 2 & 3.

Makayli Matias

Secretary | matias85@rowan.edu

Makayli Matias is a sophomore studying Vocal Music Education. She is thrilled to serve as Secretary for both Rowan’s NAfME and ACDA chapters this year, managing communication and tracking point totals for each organization. Makayli sings with both the Concert Choir and Voces ensemble and is also a member of Rowan’s Opera Company. Most recently, she was seen as the Dew Fairy in Hansel and Gretel. She looks forward to continuing to grow as a musician and leader through her involvement at Rowan.
