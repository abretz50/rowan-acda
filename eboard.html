Hanna Shein — President

Hanna is a junior Vocal Performance and Music Education double major, passionate about choral arts. She has performed with NJ All State Mixed Choir, NAfME All Eastern Treble Choir, the Honors Performance Series at Carnegie Hall, and more. Favorite memories include performing at Carnegie Hall and with Chanticleer. She recently appeared as Hansel in Rowan Opera Company’s Hansel and Gretel. When not singing, she’s reading or hanging with her cat, Luna.
