About the Chapter

Rowan ACDA supports choral music on campus and in the community through inclusive programming, hands‑on learning, and service.

  • Student-led concerts and community performances
  • Guest clinicians and professional development
  • Leadership and conducting labs

Mission

To inspire teaching, performance, and leadership through welcoming, student‑led activities that develop real‑world choral skills.

All majors welcome—no audition required for chapter membership.

Membership at Rowan

Membership in the Rowan University chapter of ACDA is free for all Rowan students and faculty. To become a chapter member, register on ProfLink.

For access to full national benefits (publications, conferences, and more), consider joining ACDA National as a Collegiate member at acda.org.

Membership FAQ

Do I need to be a music major?

No. All Rowan students are welcome. Many members are education, performance, engineering, or other majors who love choral music.

Are there dues?

Chapter membership is free. National ACDA membership is optional but recommended for conference access and publications.

What’s the time commitment?

Flexible. Come to meetings, help at events, and participate as your schedule allows. We post opportunities on the Events page.

How do I add ACDA to my résumé?

After joining on ProfLink, list “Rowan ACDA — Member” under Activities. Conference attendance and officer roles can be listed as separate entries.
