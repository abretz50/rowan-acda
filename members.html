About the Chapter

Rowan ACDA is a student-run community where singers, future educators, and developing musicians grow together. We rehearse, read new music, host conducting labs, and invite guest clinicians to share practical tools you can use in choral career.

Whether you’re preparing for a career in music or simply love to sing, you’ll find welcoming rehearsals, hands-on workshops, and plenty of ways to serve the campus and local community.

Mission

To cultivate choral artistry, leadership, and service by providing inclusive music-making, practical training, and professional connections for all Rowan students.

FAQ

  • Do I have to be a music major? No! Everyone is welcome!
  • Do I have to audition? No audition is required for membership; select performances may have auditions for parts.
  • How often do you meet? Weekly during the semester (Fridays at 3:00pm in Wilson 107).

Events & Activities

We plan a balanced mix of artistry, growth, and service throughout the year:

  • Professional Developments: guest speakers, pedagogy talks, leadership opportunites, and career Q&A.
  • Musicianship: sight-reading sessions, repertoire swaps, and peer conducting labs.
  • Service & Outreach: campus collaborations, conferences, and community performances.
  • Socials & Performances: cabaret, student conducting showcase, and chapter social events.
  • Conferences: opportunities to attend ACDA conferences with the chapter.

Benefits

  • Leadership and conducting opportunities
  • Access to professional developments, reading sessions, and new repertoire
  • Service experiences and campus/community networking
  • Preparation for conference attendance and future choral experiences

ACDA National Membership Categories

ACDA offers several membership types. Chapter participation at Rowan is open to all students; national membership is encouraged for those pursuing choral leadership or education.

  • Student: discounted membership with access to publications, resources, and conference rates.
  • Active: full membership for educators/conductors with voting privileges and full benefits.
  • Associate: for supporters and non-teaching professionals who want to stay connected.

Not sure which is right for you? Talk with an E-Board member—we’re happy to help.
