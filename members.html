About the Chapter

We build musicianship and leadership through rehearsals, workshops, and service projects. Whether you’re a singer, conductor, educator, or just curious—there’s a place for you.

Mission

To support the growth of choral musicians and educators at Rowan through community, artistry, and service.

Membership at Rowan

Attend meetings, participate in events, and get involved with committees that match your interests.

FAQ

  • Do I have to be a music major? No—everyone is welcome.
  • Do I have to audition? No audition for membership; some performances may audition.
  • How often do you meet? Weekly during the semester.
  • Can I drop in? Yes—drop‑ins are welcome at general meetings.
  • Do I need to read music? No, we’ll help you grow your skills.
