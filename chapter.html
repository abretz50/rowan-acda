Membership

Membership in the Rowan University chapter of ACDA is free for all Rowan University students and faculty. To become a member, please register on Proflink.

  • Spring Student Conducting Concert

ACDA National Membership

To access the full benefits of ACDA membership, register on the ACDA national website and pay the membership dues.

Membership Categories

  • Active

    Choral professionals and composers in the U.S. or Canada — $125

  • Retired

    Retired from all paid choral positions — $45

  • Collegiate

    Enrolled students — $15

  • Associate

    Online membership for people not in conducting, composition, education, or administration — $45

  • International

    Members living outside the United States and Canada — $45

Benefits of Membership

  • Connection to choral colleagues and professionals
  • Tools and resources to support you and your choirs
  • Professional development for conductors and educators
  • Access to national, regional, and state conferences and workshops
  • Subscription to Choral Journal and other publications
  • Access to ACDA’s online community, ChoralNet
