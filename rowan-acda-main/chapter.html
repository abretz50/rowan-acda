About the Chapter

Rowan ACDA supports choral music through education, performance, and service.

Mission

To inspire teaching, performance, and leadership through inclusive programming.

Membership

Membership in the Rowan University chapter of ACDA is free for all Rowan University students and faculty. To become a member, please register on Proflink.

Chapter Events and Activities

Our chapter is proud to host several events and activities throughout the year, including:

  • Tenor Bass Festival, hosted by Dr. Thomas each fall
  • High School ACDA Festival in the spring
  • Student Cabaret
  • Spring Student Conducting Concert

Upcoming Events

This year, our chapter plans to attend the ACDA Summer Conference, where we will have the opportunity to learn from renowned clinicians and network with other choral professionals.

ACDA National Membership

To access the full benefits of ACDA membership, you need to register on the ACDA national website and pay the membership dues.

ACDA Membership Categories

The following membership categories are available:

  • Active (choral professionals and composers in the U.S. or Canada): $125
  • Retired (retired from all paid choral positions): $45
  • Student: $15
  • Associate (online membership for people not in conducting, composition, education, or administration): $45
  • International (live outside the United States and Canada): $45

Benefits of ACDA Membership

As a member of ACDA, you'll have access to:

  • Connection to choral colleagues and professionals
  • Access to tools and resources to help you and your choirs
  • Support for your professional development as a conductor and educator
  • Opportunities to attend national, regional, and state conferences and workshops
  • Subscription to the Choral Journal and other online publications
  • Access to the ACDA online community, ChoralNet
  • And more!
