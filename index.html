More About Rowan ACDA

Founded with the mission of connecting students through music, Rowan ACDA fosters leadership and artistry. We offer masterclasses, socials, and community outreach events throughout the year. Whether you want to learn about conducting, network with peers, or just sing—this is the place for you.

About Rowan ACDA

Rowan ACDA is one of the most active collegiate ACDA chapters in the region. We participate in conferences, host our own Cabaret, and bring in guest clinicians. Membership provides both community and professional growth.
