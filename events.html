Mozart’s Requiem: Concert Choir & Symphony Orchestra

Type: Performance | Date: Sat, Dec 6, 2025 | Time: 7:30pm - 9:30pm

The Rowan University Concert Choir and Symphony Orchestra come together for a performance of one of the most celebrated works in classical music: Mozart’s Requiem. This masterpiece, filled with drama, beauty, and emotional depth, remains one of Mozart’s most enduring legacies. Under the direction of Dr. Christopher Thomas, this collaboration promises to be a moving and unforgettable evening of music.

Choral Conducting 101 (with Dr. Christopher Thomas)

Type: PD | Date: Fri, Nov 14, 2025 | Time: 3:00pm - 4:00pm

Ever wondered what it takes to lead a choir? Rowan’s own Dr. Christopher Thomas will guide us through the fundamentals of choral conducting, from gestures and communication to rehearsal strategies. You’ll gain insight into what makes a conductor effective and even have the chance to try it yourself. Whether you see conducting in your future or just want to experience music from a new angle, this is a session you won’t want to miss.

Cabaret Dress Rehearsal

Type: Dress Rehearsal | Date: Fri, Oct 10, 2025 | Time: 3:00pm - 5:00pm

Our Cabaret Dress Rehearsal is the last opportunity for performers to run through their acts in full before the show. This rehearsal is designed to help you feel comfortable on stage, practice transitions, and receive any last-minute feedback. We’ll also take time to rehearse our group song together so everyone feels confident and performance-ready. It’s a supportive environment to fine-tune your work and make those final adjustments that will bring our annual Cabaret to life!

Church Gig 101 & Cabaret Rehearsal #2

Type: General Meeting | Date: Fri, Oct 3, 2025 | Time: 3:00pm - 4:00pm

If you’ve ever wondered how church gigs work, this session is for you! We’ll cover the basics of sight singing, service structure, and professional etiquette, plus tips for success from those who’ve done it before. Whether you’re interested in picking up church jobs or want to strengthen your skills for future opportunities, you’ll leave with practical knowledge and confidence. Afterward, we’ll rehearse our group piece for the upcoming Cabaret!
