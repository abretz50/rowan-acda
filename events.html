Sight Singing Musical Theatre & Cabaret Rehearsal

General Meeting • Fri, Sep 12, 2025 • 3:00pm–4:00pm

More info

Join us for a fun session of sight singing where we’ll explore ensemble harmonies from well-loved musical theatre repertoire. Whether you’re new to sight singing or just want more practice, this is a great way to build confidence in a relaxed setting. Afterward, we’ll rehearse our group piece for the upcoming Cabaret, so come ready to sing, learn, and connect with others!
