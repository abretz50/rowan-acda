The Organ Program Curriculums

Options include BA, MA, and Minor/CUG (Certificate of Undergraduate Study).

BA in Music — Organ

  • Private study in organ literature, technique, and service playing
  • Keyboard skills, continuo/figured-bass fundamentals
  • Recital/performance opportunities and collaborative projects

MA in Music — Organ

  • Advanced repertoire and historical performance practice
  • Research/writing or additional recital track
  • Pedagogy and leadership opportunities (studio/classroom)

Minor / CUG in Organ

  • Flexible pathway to add organ proficiency to your degree
  • Focus on hymn-playing, registration, and service leading
  • Ideal for music ed, composition, and performance majors
